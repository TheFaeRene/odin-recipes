Baked Mac and Cheese


Description

I'm not quite sure when my mom started making this
recipe but I was young enough to not have had homemade
mac and cheese before. I made a few changes due to
preference but otherwise, this is my mom's mac and cheese recipe.

Ingredients

  • Spiral Noodles
  • Velveeta or off brand cheese block
  • Bread crumbs
  • Half and Half
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cheddar
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Cut cheese block into one inch cubes
  3. Melt butter and add flour, whisk constantly so butter doesn't burn
  4. Add milk and keep whisking, will thicken. Add salt and pepper
  5. Add cheese cubes and whisk until melted
  6. Drain noodles and add cheese sauce. Add a slash of half and half if too thick. Stir in cheddar
  7. Mix bread crumbs, parmesan and melted butter in separate bowl
  8. Pour mac and cheese into oven safe dish, top with breadcrumbs and bake
