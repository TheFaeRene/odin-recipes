Frozen Dark Chocolate Bananas


Description

This is a fairly simple recipe, just a bit messy and
requires a bit of patience. If you don't mind a
bit of a mess, it can be fun for kids. It can also be used
for other fruit, frozen is more of a preference.

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate chips(60% or higher)
  • Bananas
  • Coconut Oil
  • Optional toppings: sprinkles, nuts, flakey salt, shredded coconut

Steps

  1. Slice bananas into about half an inch thick pennies.
    Thick enough to get a good bite but not too thick to bite through once frozen.
  2. Put chocolate chips and coconut oil in glass bowl and place over a pot of water on stove top
    (Or melt in microwave but will have to keep reheating. 30 second intervals to keep from burning)
  3. Line cookie sheet with parchment paper
  4. Carefully stab banana slice with fork, dip in chocolate, turn to coat, let excess drip off and use
    second fork to push banana slice off onto parchment lined sheet.
    Make sure slices don't touch.
  5. Add toppings before chocolate sets.
    Repeat until all slices are covered and pop into freezer.
    Transfer into container once frozen soild.
  6. Can be left out briefly before eating to thaw slightly.
