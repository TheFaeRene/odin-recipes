Chicken Salad


Description

I initially followed a recipe for a picnic
and have altered it over the years until it become
it's own recipe. A quite requested one as well.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vanilla Greek Yogurt
  • Dried Cranberries
  • Red Grapes
  • Mayo
  • Miracle Whip
  • Walnuts

Steps

  1. Boil chicken (Can used canned chicken)
  2. Mix together greek yogurt, mayo and miracle whip and set aside
  3. Let chicken cool for ten minutes before shredding then let cool further
  4. Slice grapes in half
  5. Roughly chop walnuts
  6. Add yogurt mixture to chicken
  7. Fold in grapes, cranberries and walnuts
  8. Store in a sealed container for up to 3 days in the fridge (if it lasts that long)
